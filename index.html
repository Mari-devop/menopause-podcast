Our Mission

Rebranding Menopause aims to transform the conversation around menopause by uncovering its secrets, and misinformation of the past, managing its symptoms, and celebrating the superpowers it brings. We are committed to empowering women of all ages through education, personal stories, and expert insights—creating a community and open and unleashed conversation where women take control of their journey with confidence.

Meet Our Hosts

Sharon Grasso

Sharon Grasso, LE, CLT, Celebrity Beauty Expert and Founder of Permanent Touch Cosmetics, brings 20+ years in innovative beauty and skincare. A global leader in aesthetics, she empowers women to confidently navigate menopause’s physical changes.

Dana Persico

Dana Persico, a beauty and wellness expert in Washington, DC, and Long Island, founded Long Island Nail Skin & Hair Institute. With 25+ years in the industry, she champions women’s wellness and education, making her an ideal guide for navigating menopause on the podcast.

Ingrid Dodd

Ingrid Dodd, a publicist and digital marketing expert with 25+ years of experience, is known for her bold voice and strategic insights. She’s passionate about normalizing menopause conversations, offering women a platform to feel seen and heard.

Our Values

Empowerment Through Knowledge

Rebranding menopause provides women with accurate information, debunking myths and removing stigma, so they can navigate this life stage confidently and make informed health choices.

Promoting Health and Wellness

By addressing the real physical and emotional impacts of menopause, we encourage better healthcare support, self-care, and proactive wellness practices for women.

Celebrating Strength and Resilience

Menopause isn’t just a phase to endure-it’s a time that can reveal new strengths. Rebranding shifts the focus from loss to growth, embracing the unique capabilities women gain.

Creating an Inclusive Community

Open dialogue around menopause fosters connection, reduces isolation, and builds a supportive community where women can share experiences, find validation, and thrive together.
